### Hybrid Casual Design (2026 Playbook)

The four-layer model that's generating 100% YoY IAP growth:

1. **10-Second Hook** — mechanic anyone grasps instantly (Pixel Flow = pigs shooting colored balls)
2. **Session Design** — 3-6 min sessions with purchasable moments (extra tray slot, continue after near-miss)
3. **Meta Layer** — progression, collections, events, passes (primary reason to return by D3-D5)
4. **Economy** — dual-currency, $0.99-$2.99 starter packs, $4.99-$9.99 bundles, $7.99-$19.99 passes

**Key insight**: "The puzzle structure itself was designed for purchasable moments. The tray system is an active management tool, not a hidden penalty." — Deconstructor of Fun on Pixel Flow
